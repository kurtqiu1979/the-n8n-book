If nodes are the muscles, expressions are the nervous system — they let your data flow between nodes and shape-shift on demand.

## What you'll learn

1. **`{{ }}` expression syntax** — when to use it; toggling between "fixed value" and "expression" modes
2. **Upstream data references** — `$json` / `$('NodeName')` / `.first()` / `.all()` / `.last()`
3. **Common JavaScript** — string, date, and array operations cheat sheet
4. **Type conversion & dates** — strings ↔ numbers ↔ booleans ↔ dates
5. **The 5 traps every beginner hits** — wrong `item` reference, expression vs literal, `.item` misuse, and more
