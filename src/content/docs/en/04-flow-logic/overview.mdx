<span class="editorial-label">Part 4 · Overview</span>

The watershed between beginner and intermediate. By the end of this part, your workflows handle the hard real-world cases: *what if, otherwise, what about errors, how do I loop, how do I modularize*.

## What you'll learn

1. **IF / Switch** — conditional branching
2. **Merge — explained** — merge modes with diagrams (this is the #1 trap for beginners)
3. **SplitInBatches** — batching and rate-limiting (so you don't blow up third-party APIs)
4. **Loops** — common looping patterns
5. **Error handling** — Continue On Fail, Error Trigger, Error Workflow
6. **Sub-workflows** — reuse workflows like functions
