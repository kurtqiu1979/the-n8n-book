> **Difficulty** 🟢 Easy · **Time** ~20 min · **Core nodes** Gmail × Aggregate × AI

100+ unread emails every morning — even sorting them is exhausting. This workflow scans yesterday's new emails, AI sorts into "🔥 actionable / 📰 informational / 🗑 ignorable," DMs you on Slack — **3-minute daily inbox**.

## 📋 Key configs

<TerminalCell filename="key-config.txt" runTag="⚙">
{`Fetch Unread Emails:
  Filters: labelIds = [INBOX], q = "is:unread newer_than:1d"
  Limit:   50

Aggregate Snippets:
  Field: snippet
  → aggregate snippets from N items into one array

AI Digest:
  System: You are an email assistant. Categorize emails into 3 groups:
          - 🔥 Actionable (max 5)
          - 📰 Informational (one-liner each)
          - 🗑 Ignorable

DM to Slack:
  Channel: @you  (DM format)`}
</TerminalCell>

<TipCallout type="warn" title="Trap · Snippet too short for AI">
  
  Gmail's `snippet` is only first ~100 chars. Important emails may need full body. Either fetch `body` (10× more tokens) or pre-filter "boss/customer" senders → prioritize their emails separately.
  
</TipCallout>
