> **Difficulty** 🟡 Medium · **Time** ~25 min · **Core nodes** GCal Trigger × AI × Notion

Scrambling to draft an agenda right before a meeting? Let AI do it. Whenever Google Calendar has a new event, auto-use GPT against title+description to draft "agenda / prep notes / questions" — saves to a Notion page for you to refine.

## 📋 Key configs

<TerminalCell filename="key-config.txt" runTag="⚙">
{`GCal Trigger:
  Trigger On: eventCreated
  Calendar:   primary

AI Prep Notes:
  System: From the event title and description, generate JSON:
          { "agenda": ["..."], "prep_notes": ["..."], "questions": ["..."] }
  Response Format: json_object

Create Notion Page:
  Database: <Notion Meetings DB>
  Properties:
    Title (title):    ={{ event.summary }}
    Date (date):      ={{ event.start.dateTime }}
    Attendees (multi-select): event.attendees emails
  Blocks:
    h2: Agenda          → bulleted list from $json.agenda
    h2: Prep Notes      → bulleted list from $json.prep_notes`}
</TerminalCell>

<TipCallout type="warn" title="Trap · Private events also get analyzed">
  
  GCal Trigger fires on every new event, including private ones like "gym." AI generates meaningless agendas. **Add an IF filter:** only process events with attendees > 1 (multi-person meetings).
  
</TipCallout>

<TipCallout type="quest" title="Extensions">
  
  - Post-meeting trigger: fetch recording → AI summarize → append to same Notion page
  - Merge with history: if a weekly meeting, AI generates "compare to last week's resolutions"
  - Slack reminder: push prep notes 15 min before meeting
  
</TipCallout>
