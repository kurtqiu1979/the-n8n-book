<span class="editorial-label">Part 6 · Case 6.3 · 📣 Marketing & CRM</span>

> **Difficulty** 🟡 Medium · **Time** ~30 min · **Core nodes** Typeform × AI Tag × Salesforce

Leads collected via Typeform usually require sales to manually decide "Hot/Warm/Cold" before queuing follow-up. This workflow lets AI auto-tag, score, and estimate vertical the moment a lead lands. Salesforce receives pre-classified leads — sales queues by hotness.

## 📋 Key configs

<TerminalCell filename="key-config.txt" runTag="⚙">
{`Typeform Trigger:
  Form ID: <your Typeform>
  → fires per submission

AI Auto-Tag:
  Input:  ={{ JSON.stringify($json.answers) }}
  System: Tag + estimate vertical from Typeform answers.
          Output JSON: { tags, vertical, score(0-100) }

Create SF Lead:
  Email:    extract from typeform answers
  Company:  ...
  Industry: ={{ $json.vertical }}
  Rating:   ={{ score>70 ? 'Hot' : score>40 ? 'Warm' : 'Cold' }}

Notify Sales (Slack):
  Channel: #sales
  Text:    includes Tags + Score`}
</TerminalCell>

<TipCallout type="warn" title="Trap · Typeform's answer structure is awkward">
  
  Typeform's `answers` is an array; each has `field.ref` or `field.type` for identification. Use `.find()` by ref — never index (users may skip questions).
  
</TipCallout>
